    • how to create compelling listings

    Listings are directly related to selling products. That is why product photography using the SEO strategy is essential. SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. By using good photos, it increases the chances of a potential buyer clicking on your listing, SEO is how you get found in search results. However, it wont matter if you show up on top of the page unless you have appeasing photos that attract Etsy shoppers to click. There are many things you can do to take nice product photos. Here are some tips: use minimalistic backgrounds that doesn't clash or take attention away from the product you are trying to sell, have a cohesive profile (this makes your brand look organized, professional, and sets a foundation of style), natural lighting is the best lighting, make finetune edits to present a clean look, use very specific details in the description. Additionally it is helpful to use a mixture of descriptive and general terms in the title of your product for optimal number of searches.
